Special Tests 特殊測試 重點全考
▶
1. Cozen's Test
Cozen 測試
Tests for lateral epicondylitis（外上髁炎）
Patient: seated, arm 90° flexion, pronated, makes fist, radially deviates and extends wrist.
Examiner applies flexion force against resistance, palpates lateral epicondyle.
✓ Positive: Reproduction of lateral elbow pain
2. Reverse Cozen's Test
反向 Cozen 測試
Tests for medial epicondylitis（內上髁炎）
Patient: elbow 120° flexion, forearm supinated, wrist slightly ulnar deviated.
Examiner applies resisted flexion force, palpates medial epicondyle.
✓ Positive: Reproduction of medial elbow pain
考題 Q4 內上髁炎選用此測試
3. Middle Finger Test
(Maudsley's Test)
中指測試
Tests for lateral epicondylitis
Patient: elbow bent, palm flat. Practitioner palpates lateral epicondyle and resists patient's middle finger extension (MCP).
✓ Positive: Pain at lateral epicondyle
4. Tinel's Elbow
肘部 Tinel 測試
Tests integrity of ulnar nerve through ulnar groove（尺神經）
Tap the ulnar nerve in the groove between olecranon and medial epicondyle 4–6 times.
✓ Positive: Tingling in ulnar distribution of forearm/hand
考題 Q8 不是測試內上髁炎
5. Froment's Sign
Froment 徵
Tests for ulnar nerve weakness (adductor pollicis)（尺神經）
Patient grips paper between thumb and index; examiner pulls paper away.
✓ Positive: Thumb flexes (FPL compensation) = positive
6. Valgus Stress Test
外翻應力測試
Tests integrity of medial collateral ligament (MCL)
Elbow ~20° flexion; apply valgus force.
✓ Positive: Pain or excessive laxity on medial side
7. Varus Stress Test
內翻應力測試
Tests integrity of lateral collateral ligament (LCL)
Apply varus force at 5–30° flexion.
✓ Positive: Pain or excessive laxity on lateral side

Conditions 疾病 考試重點
▶
⭐ Lateral Epicondylitis（外上髁炎 / Tennis Elbow 網球肘）
PathophysiologyOveruse of common extensor tendon & extensor carpi radialis brevis → micro tears → fibrosis
過度使用伸肌總腱及橈側腕短伸肌 → 微撕裂 → 纖維化
SubjectiveLateral elbow pain, no trauma, pain with grip, weak grip
外側肘痛、無外傷、握持痛、握力弱
Special TestsCozen's test, Middle finger extension test
Diff DxOA, fracture, radiculopathy, radial nerve compression, fibromyalgia
TreatmentRICE, counter-force elbow brace, trigger points, acupuncture (local He-sea pts), US, TENS
🏌️ Medial Epicondylitis（內上髁炎 / Golfer's Elbow 高爾夫球肘）
PathophysiologyRepetitive overuse of forearm flexors & pronator muscles → micro tears, fibrosis
前臂屈肌及旋前肌反覆過度使用
Special TestReverse Cozen's Test
Diff DxFracture, OA, UCL injury, C/S radiculopathy, ulnar neuropathy, TOS
⚠️ Elbow Subluxation（肘關節半脫位 / Nursemaid's Elbow）
SynonymToddler elbow, Babysitter elbow 幼兒肘
PathophysiologyRadial head slips under the annular ligament from sudden pull on extended pronated arm
橈骨頭從伸展旋前的手臂突然被拉扯時滑入環狀韌帶下方
SubjectiveYoung child, no trauma, sudden arm non-use, snapping/popping, arm-pulling incident
PositionProtective: elbow flexed ~20°, partially pronated
TreatmentRefer — outside scope of practice. Post-reduction: Qi & Blood stasis Tx
🏈 Elbow Sprain（肘關節扭傷）
PathophysiologyRepetitive stress or single traumatic valgus force → UCL most commonly affected
反覆應力或單次外翻暴力 → 尺側副韌帶最常受影響
SubjectiveMedial elbow pain when throwing; acute onset during single throw
Special TestsValgus stress test, Tinel's sign for elbow
Note⭐ Baseball scenario: acute onset = Sprain, not epicondylitis (overuse)

▶
Phalen's Test
+ Reverse Phalen's
Phalen / 反向 Phalen
Tests for Carpal Tunnel Syndrome（腕管綜合症）
Phalen: max wrist flexion, dorsal hands together, hold 30–60 sec.
Reverse: max extension, palms together, hold 30–60 sec.
✓ Positive: Paresthesia in median nerve distribution (digits 1–4)
Tinel's Test (wrist)
腕部 Tinel 測試
Tests for Carpal Tunnel Syndrome
Tap over carpal tunnel (≈ PC7) for 30 sec.
✓ Positive: Paresthesia along median nerve (digits 1–4)
考題 Q1 CTS 三大測試：Phalen + Reverse Phalen + Tinel
Finkelstein's Test
Finkelstein 測試
Tests for De Quervain's Tenosynovitis（狹窄性腱鞘炎）
Patient tucks thumb in fist → therapist passively ulnar deviates the wrist.
✓ Positive: Sharp pain along distal radial forearm (LI5 area)
考題 Q4 被動尺偏！不是橈偏
Scaphoid Compression Test
舟狀骨壓迫測試
Tests for Scaphoid fracture（舟狀骨骨折）
Stabilize above wrist; apply pressure through 1st metacarpal toward radial bone (LI5 area).
✓ Positive: Pain at scaphoid

Special Tests 特殊測試 重點全考
▶
Trendelenburg Test
Trendelenburg 測試
Tests for gluteus medius weakness / tear（臀中肌無力）
Patient stands; shifts weight to one leg and lifts other leg. Observe pelvis level.
✓ Positive: Pelvis drops on unsupported side = weak stance-side glute med.
考題 Q5 陽性 = 臀中肌無力
FABER Test
(Figure 4 Test)
FABER 測試
Evaluates hip dysfunction and/or SI joint dysfunction（髖 / 骶髂關節）
Patient supine: Flex, ABduct, Externally Rotate hip (Figure 4). Stabilize opposite ASIS, apply downward force on thigh.
Ipsilateral pain = hip; Contralateral pain = SI joint.
✓ Positive: Ipsilateral OR contralateral pain reproduced
考題 Q3 = hip dysfunction AND/OR SI joint dysfunction
Scour Test
(Grind Test)
Scour 測試
Tests for osteoarthritis and acetabular dysfunction
Patient supine, hip/knee flexed; apply downward force through femur and circular motion (IR/ER).
✓ Positive: Pain, grinding sensation/sound
Thomas Test
Thomas 測試
Tests for hip flexor contracture + ITB tightness
Patient semi-supine at table edge; bring both knees to chest → release one leg to extend.
✓ Positive for hip flexors: leg cannot reach parallel to table ✓ Positive for ITB: leg externally rotates or abducts
考題 Q2 Thomas = Tight IT band + Tight hip flexors
Straight Leg Raise (SLR)
直腿抬高測試
Tests for lumbar disc herniation / sciatica
Pain 30–70°: L4–S1 disc herniation.
Pain <30°: acute spondylolisthesis, disc protrusion/extrusion, tumor
Pain >70°: hamstring/glute tightness, hip/SI pathology
✓ Positive: Neurologic leg + low back pain 30–70°
Piriformis Test
梨狀肌測試
Tests impact of piriformis on sciatic nerve
Sidelying, symptomatic side up; 60° hip flexion, 90° knee flexion. Downward pressure on lateral knee.
✓ Positive: Buttock pain or neurological signs down leg
考題 Q7 Sciatica confirmation: SLR + Piriformis Test
Ober's Test
Ober 測試
Tests for tight TFL / IT Band
Sidelying; abduct/extend hip 15°, slowly lower leg toward table.
✓ Positive: Leg does not lower to neutral/parallel
90-90 Test
90-90 測試
Tests for hamstring tightness
Supine, hip & knee both 90°; ask to extend knee.
✓ Positive: Cannot extend knee within 20° of full extension
⭐ Anterior Hip Pain — Best Tests（考題 Q6）
FABER Test + Scour Test + Thomas Test（不是 Piriformis, Trendelenburg, 90-90）
前側髖痛三大測試

Special Tests 特殊測試 重點全考
▶
Patellar Apprehension Test
髕骨恐懼測試
Tests for patellar instability（髕骨不穩定）
Apply lateral force on patella in full extension → patient slowly flexes to 90°.
Repeat with medial force (part 2).
✓ Positive: Part 1 = pain/apprehension; Part 2 = relief
考題 Q6 測試 patellar instability，不是 fracture 或 chondromalacia
McMurray / Ege's Test
McMurray 測試
Tests for meniscal tear（半月板撕裂）
Toes out (ER) + squat = medial meniscus; Toes in (IR) + squat = lateral meniscus.
✓ Positive: Pain, clicking or locking
Apley's Test
Apley 壓縮/分離測試
Prone, knee 90°. Distraction test → ligament injury. Compression test → meniscal injury.
✓ Compression more painful = meniscal. Distraction more painful = ligamentous.
Anterior Drawer Test
前抽屜測試
Tests for ACL pathology（前十字韌帶）
Knee 90°; pull tibia anteriorly. Watch for sag sign (falsely positive if PCL torn).
✓ Positive: Empty end feel or excessive anterior movement
考題 Q7
Posterior Drawer Test
後抽屜測試
Tests for PCL pathology（後十字韌帶）
Knee 90°; push tibia posteriorly.
✓ Positive: Lack of end feel or excessive posterior translation
Valgus Stress Test
外翻應力測試
Tests MCL integrity（內側副韌帶）
Knee 30° flexion, apply valgus force.
✓ Positive: Medial pain or excessive gapping
Varus Stress Test
內翻應力測試
Tests LCL integrity（外側副韌帶）
✓ Positive: Lateral pain or excessive gapping

Conditions 疾病 考試重點
▶
⚠️ "Terrible Triad" 悲慘三聯征 考題 Q1
ACL + Medial Meniscus + MCL
前十字韌帶 ＋ 內側半月板 ＋ 內側副韌帶
Mechanism: valgus force（外翻暴力）
⭐ ACL vs PCL — Key Differences
FeatureACL 前十字韌帶PCL 後十字韌帶
FunctionPrevents anterior tibial translation 防前向移位Prevents posterior tibial translation 防後向移位 考題Q5
MechanismSudden deceleration + turningDirect force on anterior tibia with flexed knee 膝前著地 考題Q4
Special TestAnterior DrawerPosterior Drawer + Sag sign
Instability withExtension (heel strike, descending stairs)Flexion (deep knee bend)
🔵 ITB Syndrome（髂脛束綜合症）
Key musclesTFL (Tensor Fascia Latae) + Gluteus Medius 考題 Q3
闊筋膜張肌＋臀中肌（不是股四頭肌）
PresentationDiffuse lateral knee pain after activity; worse uphill/stairs; popping noise
Special TestOber's test
🟢 Meniscal Injury（半月板損傷）考題 Q2
Caused by repetitive rotational / twisting forces with weight bearing（負重旋轉）
Symptoms: joint line pain, popping, locking/catching, pain with weight-bearing
Special Tests: McMurray, Apley compression/distraction, MRI
🩰 ITB Ballet Case（考題 Q8）
Clicking lateral knee, worse with stairs/ballet, weakness on resisted hip abduction → IT Band
外側膝彈響＋抗阻髖外展無力 → 髂脛束問題

踝部前抽屜測試
Tests ATFL / ankle stability. Knee 20° flexed, ankle plantarflexed 10–15°. Pull heel anteriorly.
✓ Positive: >1 cm anterior translation = ATFL rupture
Inversion Talar Tilt
距骨內翻傾斜測試
Tests calcaneofibular ligament (CFL). Pull ankle into inversion.
✓ Positive: Pain, clunking or excessive translation

Thompson 擠壓測試
Tests integrity of Achilles tendon. Prone; squeeze calf; look for plantarflexion.
✓ Positive: No plantarflexion = Achilles rupture

Hoffa 測試

✓ Positive: Injured side feels less tight; pain on calcaneus
Calcaneal Squeeze Test
跟骨擠壓測試
Tests for calcaneal injury (stress fracture, spur)
✓ Positive: Pain in calcaneus on squeezing

Conditions 疾病 考試重點
▶
⭐ Plantar Fasciitis（足底筋膜炎）考題 Q1
Key muscle groupCalves (gastrocnemius + soleus) are the most important to treat
小腿肌肉（腓腸肌＋比目魚肌）是最重要的治療目標
Pain patternWorse with prolonged inactivity（長時間休息後加重）, better with mild activity, then worsens
Rule outCalcaneal fracture as cause of pain（應排除跟骨骨折）
Location1–2 cm distal to calcaneus
⚠️ Achilles Tendon Rupture Signs（考題 Q2）
Loud popping sound at moment of rupture 斷裂時巨響
Bulging of muscle tissue at middle of calf 小腿中段肌肉膨出
Not: radiating foot pain, not knee pain
3 stages: Peritendonitis → Tendinosis → Rupture
Special Test: Thompson's Test（Thompson 測試陽性 = 完全斷裂）
🔵 Calcaneodynia（跟骨痛）考題 Q4
Most likely cause: Thinning or loss of heel fat pad（足跟脂肪墊萎縮）
Not sedentary lifestyle, not calf strain, not previous sprain
Treatment: Reduce activity or provide more heel cushioning; periosteal needling 2Hz
🟢 Inversion Ankle Sprain（踝關節內翻扭傷）
Affects lateral ligaments: ATFL most commonly
Also affects peroneal muscles (evertors)
Special Tests: Anterior Drawer + Inversion Talar Tilt (carefully)
Grades: I (stretch), II (partial tear), III (complete tear → refer)
